About Us

Prince Dance Group, A Dance Troupe Based In Berhampur Led By T. Krishna Mohan Reddy.

Prince Dance Group Is Rooted In A Small Town Of Berhampur,In The Eastern Coastline Of India, In The State Of Odisha.

Our Group Is Composed Of Twenty-Six Daily Wageworkers Who Enthralled The Entire India With Their Fantastic Performance – Krishna Act And Went On Becoming The Winner Of The Reality –Talent Show, "INDIAS GOT TALENT", With No Formal Training In Dance.
