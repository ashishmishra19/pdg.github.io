About Us

Prince Dance Group, A dance troupe based in Berhampur led by T. Krishna Mohan Reddy.

The prince dance group is based in the eastern coastline of India, in the state of Odisha. Prince Dance Group is composed of dancers who enthralled the entire India with their fantastic performance and went on becoming the winner of the reality - talent show, "INDIA'S GOT TALENT" on an Indian TV channel Colors.

Prince Dance Group is unique and known for their quick and rapid formation based on different themes.

We have won admiration and accolades from all corners of the country. Our group has perormed in big events like opening ceremony of IPL, Hockey, World Cup, Award functions and many more. Our group has performed globally and won hearts.
